About PawPortal

Dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming dogs in Valenzuela City

Our Mission

PawPortal works in partnership with the Valenzuela City Veterinary Office to provide a safe haven for abandoned and rescued dogs. Our mission is to give every dog a second chance at life by finding them loving, permanent homes while promoting responsible pet ownership in our community.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every dog has a loving home, and no animal suffers from neglect or abandonment. Through education, compassion, and dedicated rescue efforts, we strive to create a more humane society for all animals.

What We Do

Rescue & Rehabilitation

We rescue dogs from the streets, shelters, and difficult situations. Each dog receives medical care, proper nutrition, and behavioral training to prepare them for adoption.

Adoption Services

We carefully match dogs with suitable families through our adoption process, ensuring both the dog and adopter are prepared for a successful lifelong relationship.

Community Education

We promote responsible pet ownership through educational programs, workshops, and outreach activities in Valenzuela City and surrounding areas.
