Our Mission

PawPortal works with the Valenzuela City Veterinary Office to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs in need. We believe every dog deserves a loving home and a second chance at life. Through our adoption program, we've successfully matched hundreds of dogs with caring families.

Why Adopt From Us?

Health Checked

All our dogs are vaccinated, spayed/neutered, and receive comprehensive health screenings before adoption.

Lifetime Support

We provide ongoing support and guidance to ensure a successful transition for both you and your new pet.

Perfect Match

Our team helps match you with a dog that fits your lifestyle, ensuring a happy, lasting relationship.
